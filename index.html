About

Italian musician, composer, and sound designer, known for his work in experimental music and sound art. He has worked across various genres and styles, blending different approaches to create a unique sonic environment. His work often explores themes of sound design, multimedia, and experimental audio manipulation. While he may not be as widely known in mainstream music circles, his contributions to experimental and avant-garde music are notable.Music for film would likely involve unconventional, abstract soundscapes that are in sync with the themes and emotional tone of the visual storytelling.
